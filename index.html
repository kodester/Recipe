How to Make Zucchini Noodles with Cilantro Lime Chicken

Here's a bizare but easy recipe for you! Zucchini noodles! It may sound a bit odd but trust me, it is definitely tasty! I am not much of a cook but I am learning so when I came upon a healthy and easy recipe, I am on it! Try it out for yourself!

Ingredients

  • 3 large zucchini
  • 1 teaspoon
  • 1.5 lbs cut up boneless and skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/4 tablespoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon of any small hot pepper/jalapeno
  • 1 medium or 1/2 large lime, juice of 3/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Cooking Directions

  1. Using a spiralizer or mandoline, make zucchini noodles, transfer into a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Preheat olive oil in a large saute pan on high heat and add chicken. Saute until golden brown, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add cumin, 1/2 tsp salt and ground black pepper towards the end, stir and cook for another minute.
  4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Add zucchini noodles to pan and saute for 1.5 mintues, stirring frequently.
  6. Remove from heat and add previously cooked chicken, remaining salt, small hot peppers/jalape√±os, lime juice and cilantro.
  7. Serve
